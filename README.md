# Political Science PhD Admissions — GradCafe Data

A small, searchable dataset of **self-reported Political Science PhD admissions outcomes** from GradCafe, covering **2006–2026**.

Use it to get a rough sense of:

- when programs tend to release decisions,
- how admissions timelines vary across years,
- which programs appear most often in the data,
- and what applicants report about GPA/GRE profiles.

## What this is

This project collects and organizes admissions posts from [The GradCafe](https://www.thegradcafe.com) for Political Science PhD programs. It also builds on the public GradCafe data released by [deedy/gradcafe_data](https://github.com/deedy/gradcafe_data).

The goal is not to estimate “true” acceptance rates, but to make the information easier to explore historically.

## Important caveat

The data is **self-reported by applicants** and is therefore incomplete, noisy, and not representative of the full applicant pool.
